Vegetarian Black Bean Chili

Quick, easy, healthy... this vegetarian black bean chili, based on Panera's black bean soup, whips up in half hour or less.


Ingredients:

  • 2 Bell Peppers
  • 3 15.5 oz cans black beans
  • 1 14.5 oz can fire roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 small onion
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup vegetable stock
  • 1 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1 Tbsp cumin powder
  • Salt
  • 2 Tbsp cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in pan over medium high heat..
  2. Chop onion, peppers and garlic. Add to heated pan and sauteÃÅ 5 minutes..
  3. Add remaining ingredients. Bring to low boil, reduce heat to low. Simmer, uncovered, 20 minutes.
  4. Serve topped with sour cream or shredded cheese.
