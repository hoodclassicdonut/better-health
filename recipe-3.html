Mexican Street Corn Salad with Grilled Chicken

Mexican Street Corn Grilled Chicken Salad is a fiesta in a bowl! You will love this tangy, spicy chicken and corn salad.


Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husks removed
  • 1 ½ pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Mexican cream
  • 1 teaspoon ancho chili powder
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
  • 6 to 8 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1 to 2 cups multi-colored cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ⅓ cup crumbled cotija cheese
  • fresh chopped cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill to medium-high and lightly grease the grill grates with oil.
  2. Season the corn and the chicken thighs with salt and pepper.
  3. Transfer corn and chicken to the grill; cover and cook for 13 to 15 minutes.
  4. Cut the kernels from the cobs and place them in a bowl.
  5. To the kernels, add 2 tablespoons lime juice, minced garlic, mayonnaise, Mexican cream or yogurt, and ancho chili powder; mix and stir
    until well combined.
  6. In a small mixing bowl make the dressing by combining olive oil, lime juice, honey, salt, and pepper; whisk until thoroughly incorporated.
  7. Cut the chicken into strips.
  8. Place the mixed salad greens and cherry tomatoes in a large salad bowl and toss with the dressing.
  9. Top the salad with the corn mixture, chicken slices, and cotija cheese.
  10. Garnish with cilantro and serve.
