Moroccan Lentil Soup

Moroccan Lentil Soup is a comforting soup recipe. The warm and flavorful combination of Moroccan spices will forever change your idea of a bowl of lentils.


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Carrots
  • 2 tsp Coriander, powder
  • 4 Garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp Ginger
  • 2 cups Lentils, dried
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Potato
  • 4 cups Spinach, fresh
  • 1 14.5oz can Tomatoes
  • 4 cups Vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp Black pepper
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 2 tsp Paprika
  • 1 Salt
  • 1 tsp Turmeric, powder
  • 3 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tsp Cumin, powder
  • 1/2 cup Cream
  • 2 cups Water

Instructions:

  1. Chop your veggies and heat up some olive oil in a heavy bottomed pot over medium high heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and sauteÃÅ for a minute. Then add carrots and potatoes.
  3. Stir well, cook 2-3 minutes then add finely chopped ginger and garlic.
  4. Mix and cook 2-3 more minutes.
  5. Rinse your lentils and mix them into your pot with all of your Moroccan spices.
  6. Add tomatoes then mix and cook till they begin to break down a little, 5-8 minutes.
  7. Stir in your broth and two cups of water, let it come to a boil, then reduce heat to medium low and simmer till the lentils are cooked.
  8. Check every 10-15 minutes and give it a stir
  9. Depending upon the type of lentils, the time could vary from 45 minutes to an hour.
  10. When the lentils are done, stir in a little cream.
  11. Add in your spinach and cook till it wilts.
